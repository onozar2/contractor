How supplier accounts work

The money fact

For most building-materials wholesalers, opening a trade account is free. The gates are a business license + EIN, a CA resale/seller's-permit certificate, a signed credit application, and (for a new company) a personal guaranty.

Where the dollars go

Budget dollars for first stocking orders + dealer programs (window manufacturer dealer programs like Milgard, and tile dealer programs like Daltile Statement Dealer), not for account fees.

Acquisition angle

A target that already holds these trade accounts + dealer certifications (Milgard/Andersen certs, net-30 history, established credit) is worth a premium — those take 6-12 months + volume to build.
