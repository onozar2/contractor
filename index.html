Who We Are

Experience You Can See in the Work

Frank Nozar is the founder of Joon Development Group and a California Licensed General Contractor (Class B, #1107974) with more than two decades of hands-on experience building and managing commercial real estate across Los Angeles.

Over the course of his career, Frank built and operated 10 retail locations across the LA area — overseeing every store build-out, remodel, and site improvement personally. He knows what it costs to cut corners, so he doesn't.

For the past decade, Frank has owned and managed over 100,000 square feet of commercial real estate in Los Angeles, leveraging his contractor license to scope, bid, and execute tenant improvements, capital repairs, ADA upgrades, roofing, concrete work, and full build-outs — handling architects, engineers, permits, subcontractors, and inspections from start to finish.

When you work with Joon Development Group, you work directly with Frank. No handoffs to project managers you've never met. Just a licensed, experienced operator who treats your project like it's his own property.
