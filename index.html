Process

Clear scope. Clean communication. No mystery handoffs.

You work directly with an owner-led team that understands permits, trades, tenant needs, change orders, and the cost of slow decisions.

01 / Walkthrough

We understand the goal.

We review the property, constraints, budget range, timeline, and the reason the project matters.

02 / Scope

We price the real work.

We define materials, trades, permitting needs, unknowns, and assumptions before the job starts.

03 / Build

We coordinate the moving parts.

Subcontractors, inspections, schedule, field decisions, and change control stay organized.

04 / Closeout

We finish with accountability.

Punch list, documentation, final walkthrough, and follow-up are handled with ownership.
