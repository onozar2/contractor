FAQ

Answers homeowners and property owners usually need first.

Strong local contractor sites reduce uncertainty before the first call. These answers set expectations around scope, permits, communication, and project fit.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes. When a project requires permits, Joon coordinates the permit path, required trades, inspections, and closeout steps so the work is documented properly.

What areas do you serve?

Joon is based in Los Angeles and focuses on Southern California, including the San Fernando Valley, Ventura, and nearby Los Angeles County communities.

Can you work with existing plans?

Yes. Joon can review existing drawings, coordinate with architects or engineers, and help turn plans into a realistic scope, budget, and construction sequence.

What makes Joon different from a typical remodeler?

Frank's commercial real estate background gives each project an owner's lens: budget discipline, schedule, family impact, and long-term property value.
