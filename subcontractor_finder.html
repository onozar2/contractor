Ranked sourcing playbook

Best-first sourcing for Los Angeles subs: start where strong crews already leave evidence, then use online platforms only to fill gaps.

1 Highest ROISupplier referrals

Ask supply houses which crews have real volume, good payment habits, and net-30 accounts.

2 Field signalActive job sites

Photo trucks/signs, capture foreman numbers, and log who is working on good projects.

3 Repeat evidencePermit data

Find who repeatedly pulls permits in LADBS, Pasadena, Glendale, Santa Monica, and Beverly Hills.

4 Trust graphOther subs

When one trade is excellent, ask who they like working with across the other trades.

5 NetworkTrade associations

Use ASA, AGC California, and Builders Exchange for events, rosters, and bid-network signal.

6 VolumeOnline platforms

Use BuildingConnected, SmartBid, Houzz Pro, and Thumbtack for names, then verify hard.
