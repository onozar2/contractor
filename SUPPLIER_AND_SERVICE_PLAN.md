# Service Catalog + Subcontractor + Supplier Plan

_Reference for the Joon subcontractor-finder / GC operation. Model company: **We The People Construction** (wtpconstruction.com), the primary full-service SoCal remodeler. Every account-cost figure is marked **[CITED]** (from a distributor source) or **[EST]** (a realistic industry range where exact numbers aren't public)._

**Headline money fact:** For the large majority of building-materials wholesalers, **opening a trade account is free** — no membership/application fee. The real gates are (1) a business license + EIN, (2) a **California resale / seller's-permit certificate**, (3) a signed **credit application**, and usually (4) a **personal guaranty** for a new company. The exceptions are **window manufacturer dealer programs** (showroom + install team + volume) and a few **tile dealer programs** (participation fees). Budget dollars for **first stocking orders + dealer programs**, not for account fees.

## 3. Windows & Doors — manufacturers & wholesale distributors (SoCal)

Two paths: (a) **manufacturer certified dealer** (training + showroom + volume, best pricing), or (b) buy through a **local wholesale window distributor** on a normal trade account. _Cunningham Doors & Windows (in Deal Vault) is itself a local dealer/installer tier — it buys from the manufacturers below._

| Manufacturer / distributor | Trade accounts? | Cost / requirements | Src |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Milgard** (default SoCal vinyl/fiberglass) | Certified Dealer | Requires **retail showroom, in-house install team, CS staff**; trained/certified to sell+install. No public fee. Effective startup **~$10K–$50K+** + ongoing volume. | facts [CITED], $ [EST] |
| **Andersen** (premium wood/composite) | Certified Contractor (3 tiers) | **Factory training, install education, background check, proof of insurance**. **Cert fee waived on apply**; new certs get **$1,000 Business Development Funds**. | [CITED] |
| **Pella** (premium; Lowe's partner) | Pella Professionals | Via pro portal / local SoCal branch; license + insurance + credit app. No public fee. | [CITED]/[EST] |
| **Marvin** | Dealer network | Distributor sponsorship + credit app + resale cert. | [EST] |
| **JELD-WEN** | Tiered partner dealer (1–6) | Dealer application + resale cert + credit terms. No public fee. | tiers [CITED], $ [EST] |

## 4. Electrical wholesale / supply houses (SoCal, contractor accounts)

All open **net-30 trade accounts** to licensed electrical contractors. Standard: credit app, resale cert, trade + bank refs, personal guaranty for a new company; some want a copy of the registration/surety bond. **No fee to open.**

| Supply house | SoCal presence | Terms | Src |
|---|---|---|---|
| **CED (Consolidated Electrical Distributors)** — largest US independent | Extensive LA/OC/IE | Credit app + **current financial statement**; **contractors include copy of registration surety bond**. Pay by 15th of following month (discount if by 10th); **1½%/mo** after 30 days. | [CITED] |
| **Rexel / Platt Electric Supply** | Strong CA (Platt 135+ West-Coast branches) | Credit app, resale cert, refs, guaranty; net-30. | presence [CITED], terms [EST] |
| **Graybar** (via American Electric Supply, SoCal subsidiary) | SoCal | Credit app + resale cert + net-30; guaranty typical for new. | presence [CITED], terms [EST] |
| **Border States Electric** | Construction/industrial | Credit app; net-30; resale cert. | presence [CITED], terms [EST] |
| **Local independents** (city electric supply) | LA/OC/IE | License, resale cert, credit app, guaranty; most flexible on small first orders. | [EST] |

## 5. Other material-supplier categories (named SoCal suppliers + setup)

Baseline for all: **net-30 trade account** = credit app + CA resale cert + business license/EIN; new companies sign a personal guaranty; occasional small first-order min. **No membership/opening fee** is the norm.

**Plumbing:** Ferguson (Pro Services; credit check + refs + guaranty for start-ups) [CITED/EST] · Hajoca (resale cert on file; net-30) [CITED] · PACE Supply, Todd Pipe [EST].

**Roofing:** ABC Supply (Credit or COD app; myABCsupply) [CITED] · Beacon Building Products (branch account, net-30/COD) [CITED/EST] · SRS Distribution (credit/COD; Roof Hub) [CITED].

**Lumber / building materials:** Ganahl Lumber (OC independent; contractor charge account) [EST] · **Home Depot Pro Xtra** — free; volume pricing ~$1,500 spend; special financing $299+ [CITED] · **Lowe's Pro** — free; Platinum tier at $10K–$24,999 annual (or instant w/ Lowe's Pro card) [CITED] · FBM (drywall/lumber distributor) [CITED].

**Paint:** **Dunn-Edwards** (SoCal pro standard) — general/job/installment charge accounts; apply via Credit Dept 800-733-3866; 2% prompt-pay (2NEM) by the 15th; 1.5%/mo past-due; no membership fee [CITED] · Sherwin-Williams, Vista Paint [EST].

**Flooring / tile / stone:** **Daltile** — ProContractor program + **Statement Dealer Program** (volume + showroom space + **participation fees** for special pricing) [CITED] · **MSI (M S International)** — importer/wholesaler; trade account [CITED] · Emser Tile (LA distributor; authorized dealer) [CITED].

**HVAC:** Baker Distributing / Gemaire / Carrier Enterprise (all Watsco; largest US HVAC/R distributor) — trade accounts via credit app + resale cert [CITED/EST] · PACE Supply HVAC [CITED].

**Drywall / insulation:** **L&W Supply / "ABC Supply Interiors"** — 17 CA branches incl. Inglewood, Sun Valley, Redlands, Palm Springs; credit app [CITED] · GMS, FBM [CITED].

**Concrete / masonry / hardscape:** **RCP Block & Brick** — #1 SoCal hardscape/masonry; 6+ centers; business credit accounts [CITED] · Angelus Block (CMU/pavers; trade accounts) [CITED] · ready-mix: National Ready Mixed, CalPortland, Robertson's [EST].

**Cabinets & countertops:** Cabinets — KraftMaid, US Cabinet Depot, Fabuwood via wholesale distributors (business credential + credit app) [EST]. Countertops (slab yards) — MSI, Studio Haus (wholesale quartz), Apex Granite Outlet (LA; dedicated trade accounts), Block Tops. Requires verified trade account (fabricator/contractor credential) [CITED].

**Solar equipment:** CED Greentech, BayWa r.e., Krannich Solar + electrical supply houses; same CED-style credit app; net-30 [EST].

## 6. Cost cheat-sheet

| Category | Typical opening cost | Src |
|---|---|---|
| Standard materials trade account (plumbing, electrical, roofing, drywall, paint, lumber, tile, HVAC, concrete) | **$0 fee** — credit app + resale cert + guaranty; net-30 | CITED pattern |
| Home Depot Pro Xtra / Lowe's Pro | **$0** to join; volume pricing ~$1,500 (HD) / $10K annual (Lowe's Platinum) | CITED |
| Window mfr **dealer** (Milgard) | Showroom + install team + training; effective **~$10K–$50K+** startup | EST |
| Andersen Certified Contractor | Fee **waived on apply**; **+$1,000 BDF** welcome; training/insurance | CITED |
| Western Window Systems Registered Dealer | Credit app + resale cert; **50% deposit on custom orders** | CITED |
| Tile dealer program (Daltile Statement Dealer) | Volume + showroom space + **participation fees** | CITED |
| Slab-yard trade account | **$0 fee**; verified fabricator/contractor credential | CITED |

**Acquisition angle:** a target that **already holds these trade accounts + dealer certifications** (Milgard/Andersen certs, net-30 history, established credit lines) is worth a real premium — those take 6–12 months + volume history to build from scratch.
