## What this teaches
Confirms the same Guideline Builders exclusions boilerplate as bids #02/#03/#06/#07. The 11-step, cost-loaded milestone schedule (front-loaded at material ordering, then evenly distributed through construction phases, holding ~8% back for final inspection) is a strong template for ADU-scale payment schedules and is far more granular than the single-lump-sum or 5-stage schedules seen in the smaller remodel bids.
