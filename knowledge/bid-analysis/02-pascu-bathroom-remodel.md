## What this teaches
Confirms the **client-supplies-all-finishes, contractor-covers-all-rough-and-labor** split as the dominant CA remodeling-market pattern (matches bids #03, #05, #06, #07 almost verbatim in wording — this exclusions paragraph appears to be Guideline Builders' standard boilerplate, reused nearly word-for-word across at least 4 of their contracts in this set). The 45-day completion window and "reimburse contractor for materials purchased on client's behalf" clause are also recurring standard terms worth carrying into the Joon template.
