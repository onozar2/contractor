## Standing SoCal gotchas (cross-line)
- **CRRC / Title 24 cool roof** — roofing colors must be CRRC-listed by specific product+color (sheets 01, 02).
- **Title 24 energy code** — windows U-factor/SHGC (04), HVAC + R-8 ducting 2025 code (15), insulation minimums (17).
- **SCAQMD Rule 1121 ultra-low-NOx** — gas water heaters in the LA basin; zero-NOx phase-out in rulemaking (16).
- **NEM 3.0** — export rates gutted solar-only payback; batteries carry the economics (18).
- **SB-969** — battery-backup opener required on garage door opener replacement (22).
- **Rebate volatility** — TECH Clean CA / HEEHRA pools reported reserved; verify before quoting (15, 16).
