## Method note (for whoever continues this)

The reliable extraction method: open the video's Drive preview, click the Gemini sparkle icon (top right of the preview toolbar) to open the "Ask Gemini" panel, ask a single combined question covering products/pricing/process/objections, wait for the "Thinking..." indicator to resolve (large videos can take 40-90+ seconds), then use `read_page` (which auto-saves to a tool-results JSON file when the output is large) and the `extract-gemini.js` script in the scratchpad to pull the clean response text out of the accessibility-tree dump. About 40% of videos in this pass returned "Gemini can't summarize and answer questions about this video because captions can't be generated" — this appears file-specific (possibly tied to how/when the video was originally uploaded/encoded) rather than tied to video length, since both very short and very long videos failed and succeeded in the same session. Retrying later or via the video's own "Transcript" button (also failed on the same files) did not help.
