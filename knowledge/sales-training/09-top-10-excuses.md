Source: "FINAL 1080p 56. Top 10 Excuses.mp4" (Gemini video-analysis panel, Google Drive folder "12. Sales Materials"). No whiteboard exists for this module.

## The core rule

**The Ultimate Pricing Rule**: every excuse a client gives you means only one thing — "the price is too high" [exact quote continuation cut off by extraction, but this framing is stated directly by Gemini as the speaker's core claim]. Josh does not treat these as 10 distinct problems needing 10 distinct scripts — he treats all 10 as symptoms of the same underlying price objection.

**Value Formula**: your job is to sit down with the client and show them that the price you gave is justified/fair [exact wording cut off]. No other specific pricing numbers or formulas are given in this video (contrast with 04.06-kitchen and 10-cancellation, which do have dollar figures).
