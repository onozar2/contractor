# Other Building Materials — Factory-Direct (imported) vs Domestic Wholesale

High-container-volume materials a ~$1–2M/yr LA remodel GC might consider importing direct.
All figures **[ONLINE]** (Alibaba/Made-in-China listings, US distributor listings, Federal
Register AD/CVD orders) unless a sub gives Ori a direct number. As of 2026-07-13.

**The pattern:** raw FOB is 60–95% below domestic on paper for almost everything — but US
**antidumping/countervailing duties** have specifically targeted Chinese quartz, cabinets, and
tile at 250–725% combined, which erases the arbitrage. Lighter-duty categories (LVP/SPC
flooring, vinyl windows) are the only realistic direct-import candidates.

## LVP / SPC flooring (vinyl-core) — IMPORT NOW ✅
The strongest direct-import case in the whole set.
- FOB China $0.19–1.00/sqft; domestic wholesale $1.50–4.00/sqft.
- **No China AD/CVD** on vinyl flooring (HTS 3918); general ~40.3% stack only. Landed still
  **~50–70% cheaper** than domestic.
- 40HQ container ~7,000–13,000 sqft = under 1 year of use for a busy shop. Lead time 14–20 days.
- ⚠ **Real engineered hardwood is different** — separate Multilayered Wood Flooring AD/CVD
  order; stay domestic for that.

## Porcelain / ceramic tile — depends on origin
- FOB China $0.25–1.00/sqft vs domestic $2–5/sqft = 60–90% cheaper (widest raw spread).
- **But China ceramic-tile AD/CVD ~600–725%** (continued Feb 2026) → China is out. India also
  now AD/CVD.
- **Verdict:** verified non-China / non-AD-CVD origin → import at volume (a container ~11,000–
  14,000 sqft = 1–3 yrs). Needs storage + breakage tolerance over a 3–5 week transit.

## Quartz / porcelain slabs — mostly closed
- FOB China $2–9/sqft vs domestic $30–75/sqft (75–95% cheaper on paper).
- **China quartz AD 265–337% + CVD 45–191%** → dead. Vietnam currently exempt (~20% general)
  but a **Section 201 global safeguard (up to 50%)** cleared an ITC injury vote April 2026,
  decision mid-2026.
- **Verdict:** China stay domestic. Vietnam only at volume AND only if Joon has/partners with
  a **fabrication shop** (slabs aren't buy-and-install) — and Section 201 makes a single-
  container bet speculative right now.

## Kitchen cabinets (RTA) — STAY DOMESTIC ❌
The clearest "don't."
- FOB ~$40–100/LF vs domestic $100–300/LF (60–75% cheaper on paper).
- **China Wooden Cabinets AD/CVD up to 251%** + **Vietnam circumvention ruling** (Chinese
  components caught) + **Section 232 25% on cabinets from EVERY country** (Oct 2025, rising to
  50% Jan 2027). No low-duty sourcing country left at GC scale.

## Vinyl windows — domestic for spot buys
- FOB China ~$55–140/unit vs domestic ~$100–300/unit; duty ~22.5% (no AD/CVD on finished
  units).
- **The real gate is CA Title 24 / NFRC:** every permitted window needs an NFRC U-factor/SHGC
  label the inspector checks at final. Only a handful of Asian factories are NFRC-listed
  (multi-month, multi-$K per product line).
- **Verdict:** stay domestic for spot/project buys; import only at 50+ units/yr on ONE
  NFRC-certified line. (Matches the earlier "windows are effectively dealer-only" read.)

## Summary
| Material | Saving % pre-duty | After-duty reality | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|
| LVP/SPC flooring | 50–70% (net) | No AD/CVD; ~40% general | **Import now** (vinyl-core only) |
| Ceramic/porcelain tile | 60–90% | China AD/CVD ~600–725% | Non-China origin, at volume |
| Quartz slabs | 75–95% | China AD/CVD ~300–430% | Vietnam + fab shop, at volume; Sec.201 risk |
| Kitchen cabinets (RTA) | 60–75% | Sec.232 25→50% all countries | **Stay domestic** |
| Vinyl windows | large but erodes | ~22.5% + NFRC gate | Domestic for spot; import only at committed volume |

**Caveat:** several exact duty %s (quartz-Vietnam, tile country-by-country) are single-source
or from the post-SCOTUS-Feb-2026 regime that is still moving — verify with a customs broker
before committing capital to any container.
